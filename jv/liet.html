Overview

Language and love are celebrated in this lettering based poster!


Languages have their own personalities and manners of rolling out the tongue, and lettering can be used to make these characteristics come to life. Across all languages, ‘love’ remains a strong and present word and, in times of increasing division, understanding each other through love is the key to staying united -- we must love in every tongue.


(from top to bottom, the languages depicted are: Arabic, French, Russian, Spanish / Portuguese, and English.)

Languages when spoken carry a lot of nuances, for example; Portuguese and Spanish tend to be more flowy and accented, while Russian (to the western listener) can have sharp tones.

Through their development, each language was given a character.




French was written in smooth hand-written cursive.





Spanish / Portuguese was given a decorative touch with its serifs for a sense of connectivity.

Arabic was given sharp edges and curves.




Russian was designed with more condensed letterforms.






English had it’s letterforms played with width and length to bring “heaviness” to its look.
