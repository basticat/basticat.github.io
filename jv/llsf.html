Long Live Short Film

Brand Development // Poster Design // Illustration
Project
Process

Overview

Long Live Short Film is a film festival schedule to happen in 2020. The organizers (Beth Hall Thrasher and Jason Thrasher) managed its predecessor, the Short Attention Span Film and Video Festival.

For their new project, they wanted to incorporate the same punk, DIY, and gritty personality the previous one had, but in a new format.

Posters

In order to further develop the DIY and “punk” elements of the brand, posters were chosen as the best outlet to portray the importance of texture and media in the project.

Stickers

A set of stickers were designed to broaden the application of the brand identity and bring character to the festival, while creating something fun and interactive for its audience to take home.

The hand-painted elements are the key to the energy of the project!

They translate the DIY aspect of independent film making, while tying it back to the punk nature of the event.

To complement the organic nature of the lettering, a mix of spontaneous ink blots and colored pencil marks flowed freely through the design.

Furthermore, the sharpie illustrations bring character to the brand.
