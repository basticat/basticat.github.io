Overview

As Assistant Design Editor at the student managed Red&Black Newspaper, I had the opportunity to immerse myself in editorial design and constantly produce layouts, illustrations, data visualization, and info-graphics that were printed in 10,000 units every week.


The goal of every project was to, thought design, enhance the meaning and message of a story, and make them more engaging to the reader.

Illustrations/ Info-graphics

Illustrations are key in order to bring stories to life. They are eye catching and carry ideas that can be understood very quickly, making them a valuable asset in editorial design and journalism.

Spreads

A well designed page is crucial for the success of a newspaper. It brings illustrations, photography, and writting together to create an experience.


It allows the viewers to be more engaged and feel confortable when moving their eyes through the story. A good design can make a story much more impactful!
