Developmental Therapy Institute

Brand Development
Project
Process

Overview

The Developmental Therapy Institute is an Athens based non-profit that provides training for teachers to work with children, focusing on the ones with severe behavioral and emotional distress. The institute carries a light and approachable feel aimed at teachers while setting a child-like tone that completes the brand.

Goals

The institute had some media limitations, such as the lack of a graphic design staff and mostly black and white print material. Keeping these limitations in mind I designed brand elements that could be easily incorporated, flexible in regards to color, and that through simplicity achieved a fun and approachable feel.

The photo translates the spirit of the institute itself; friendly, understanding, and encouraging.


Much like a teacher.

How can it be made simpler?

The photo gave me a story the logo could tell, and become a compelling factor for the brand.

Gender-less, easy to replicate, shows emotion and action. Stick figures bring a human element to the design.

Arrows allow the information
to be explained in a more approachable way.

Overblown shapes set a fun tone among the academic content.
